Plugin Configuration

  1. Click on "Plugins Configuration"

  2. Select Flickr Plugin on the left and click on "Run Configuration Wizard"

  3. Click on "Next"

  4. Enter your yahoo credentials and click on "Sign In"

  5. Click on "OK, I'LL AUTHORIZE IT"

  6. Copy the pin and paste it in the PIN input field. Then click "Finish"

  7. Select the Instagram plugin from the left and clik on "Run Configuration Wizard"

  8. Click on "Open in browser"

  9. Authenticate to instagram with your credentials

  10. You are redirected to www.geocreepy.com, copy your token value

  11. Back in the Instagram wizard window, paste your token value and click on "Finish"

  12. Select the Twitter plugin from the left and clik on "Run Configuration Wizard"

  13. Click on "Next"

  14. Authenticate with Twitter and click on "Authorize app"
  15. Copy the PIN and paste it in the PIN input field. Then click "Finish"
