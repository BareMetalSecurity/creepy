Privacy Policy

General

No logs are kept for the request to http://www.geocreepy.com/get_token.html where you get the code to complete the plugin configuration. The page is stored on Github pages and all the processing is made in Javascript in your browser as you can see in https://github.com/jkakavas/creepy/blob/gh-pages/get_token.html.

Information Collection

cree.py runs on your device, no data is gathered about you or your use.
